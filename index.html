Plated Service

Plated service is a more formal dining experience often seen at weddings and upscale events. Guests are seated, and servers bring pre-plated meals to each guest. It allows for precise portion control and presentation, making it an elegant choice. Each course is typically served sequentially, including appetizers, salads, entr√©es, and desserts.

Family Style

Family-style catering encourages a sense of community and sharing. Large platters of food are placed on tables, and guests pass them around to serve themselves. It creates a communal dining experience and encourages interaction among guests. It's suitable for casual gatherings, such as reunions or rustic-themed events..
